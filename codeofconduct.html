Code of Conduct

Upholding Our Code of Conduct

At Love2Coach, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and inclusivity. We expect all users of our platform (www.love2coach.io) to adhere to this Code of Conduct to ensure a safe, respectful, and supportive environment for everyone participating in our community, whether online or during any other Love2Coach events.

The Pledge

We pledge to create a welcoming and harassment-free experience for all individuals using Love2Coach, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, nationality, physical appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

The Standards

Positive Behavior

We encourage all users to contribute to a positive and inclusive community by:

  • Using welcoming and inclusive language
  • Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
  • Graciously accepting constructive feedback
  • Referring to individuals by their preferred pronouns, using gender-neutral language when unsure

Unacceptable Behavior

The following actions are not tolerated on Love2Coach:

  • Trolling, insulting remarks, or harassment in any form (public or private)
  • Sharing personal or confidential information without consent
  • Ignoring clear communication boundaries, such as requests to stop or disengage
  • Using sexualized language or imagery, or engaging in unwelcome sexual advances
  • Offensive or disturbing language or imagery
  • Engaging in arguments related to politics or sensitive issues
  • Promoting or encouraging inequality based on age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, nationality, race, religion, sexual orientation, or personal appearance
  • Promotion of drug use or illegal activities
  • Attacking others' personal preferences or choices
  • Any conduct that would be deemed inappropriate in a professional setting

Enforcement

Violations of the Code of Conduct can be reported by emailing us at team@love2coach.io. All reports will be thoroughly reviewed and investigated, with appropriate action taken as necessary. This may include removing comments or banning users who violate the Code. Love2Coach reserves the right to take action against any behavior that is deemed harmful, offensive, or in violation of our standards.

At Love2Coach, we empower you to achieve your goals with personalised coaching designed to fit your needs.
