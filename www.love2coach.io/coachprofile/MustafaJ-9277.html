With over 15 years of proven experience and more than 300 successful candidates coached into real roles, I am Mustafa Jama, a Certified Work Coach dedicated to helping you secure the career you have been working toward. I specialise in guiding graduates, course completers, and internationally educated professionals into job ready positions through a structured and highly effective coaching framework.

My approach is built on deep insight into what employers truly want. I focus on the four areas that consistently determine your success: gaining credible experience, creating a high impact CV that immediately captures attention, mastering confident and concise interview skills, and securing strong references from trusted organisations. Through a method known as strategic dating, I help candidates obtain validated experience and professional references that remove the typical barriers to landing early career roles.

Every part of my coaching process is personalised, practical, and results driven. I work closely with you from start to finish, refining your CV, preparing you for interviews, building your confidence, and ensuring you present yourself as a standout candidate in a competitive job market. Many of my clients secure roles within six weeks, and some even faster.

If you want a clear, guided path into your chosen career with professional support at every stage, I am here to help you make it happen.
