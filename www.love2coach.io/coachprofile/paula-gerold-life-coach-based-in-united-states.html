Paula G CERTIFIED

Life Coach

4.5 (2 reviews)

Featured Coach Rising Coach Elite Coach

I’ve known for a long time that I am a coach and trainer at heart who loves helping people have “aha moments,” so I decided to become a certified coach. I’ve been a coach and trainer throughout my career, attending training courses and a Just In Time coaching program when I worked at Optum. As part of that program, I coached more than 20 people who were working through career and personal issues. I’ve also created and led leadership trainings, DEI concepts and other soft-skill courses that helped people become better managers and leaders.

How do I what I do? By shining a light on your strengths and helping you connect with yourself. I’ll ask questions to uncover what it is that you truly want to change, and we’ll dig deep together because sometimes we think it’s one thing, but as we go further, we realize there’s more to it. I promise that everything we do will be focused on you and not on some scripted program. We’ll set small goals, that will add up to larger change, and I’ll never, ever judge you. I will be your cheerleader, your accountability partner and whatever else you might need to make the change you want, and I’ll do this in a way that works best for you.

My ideal clients are open, ready and motivated to take actions that will change their lives and are committed to their personal growth. I am selective about who I will take on as a client because we have to be the right fit. I have access to many other coaches, so if we don’t click or fit, I can find another coach better suited to helping you. One thing I will not do is try to be your therapist. I am not a licensed therapist, and I cannot diagnose, support, or treat mental health conditions. However, Aha Moments works well alongside therapy.

What I can offer you?

I have two coaching options as well as a free get-to-know you session. The Aha Moments Coaching program provides long-term support, whereas the Aha Moments Coaching session is for those items that are short-term, in the moment goals or concerns you would like to address.

  • The Open the Relationship session: the free get-to-know-you session designed to help us determine whether we're a good fit to work together.
  • The Aha Moments Coaching Program will help you discover your strengths, connect with yourself, and overcome fear to move forward and create the life you want by making small, cumulative changes over 12 weeks.
  • The Aha Moments Coaching Session is a one-on-one coaching for goals or concerns that don't need long-term support to tackle.
