You might be the only one trying in your marriage but you are not alone AND you can make a difference!

I have been where you are, I feel your pain and can truly understand. I want more than anything for you to know that you are not alone and that there is hope.


I had been praying and trying for a long time without seeing any changes. Things were so intense and I desperately wanted to be seen, heard, understood by my husband. Instead, every attempt at conversation led to arguments and disconnection.

I felt alone.

Friends were nowhere to be found. Shame gripped me. I didn’t know who I could talk to. Who could I trust?  And if by chance I found someone to talk to, who would understand?

It looked like the only way out was to embrace the pain of failure and leave. But compelled by my strong desire to honor my marriage vows and leave a different legacy for my children, I latched on to my childhood faith in one last ditch effort and thankfully I was able to discover a power within me that had been largely dormant.

That coupled with my discovery of the Life Coaching world afforded me the guidance and safe space I needed to develop new perspectives and habits that allowed me to see powerful shifts and transformation in my marriage. Get this, even though at the time it seemed like I was the only one trying!

Now it is my passion and mission to help women like YOU do the same.


My Approach

I provide a safe, empathetic, and non-judgmental space and use elements of my own experience, Scripture, prayer and professional strategies and accountability to help you shift the trajectory of your life and marriage.
