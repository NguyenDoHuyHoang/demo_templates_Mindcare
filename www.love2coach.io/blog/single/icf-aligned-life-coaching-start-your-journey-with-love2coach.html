What Is ICF Aligned Life Coaching?

The International Coaching Federation (ICF) is the leading global organisation for professional coaching. It sets the benchmark for coaching ethics, competencies, and standards. When we refer to ICF aligned life coaching, we’re talking about a coaching approach that:

  • Is client-centered and non-directive
  • Encourages deep listening, curiosity, and accountability
  • Emphasizes measurable goals and ongoing growth
  • Respects coaching ethics and confidentiality
  • Is built around the ICF’s core competencies


Even if you’re not seeking ICF credentialing right away, learning from an ICF-aligned framework gives you a professional foundation that builds credibility and helps you deliver high-quality coaching experiences.

Why Coaching Training Matters

While coaching may seem like a natural extension of helping others or offering advice, true coaching is a skill that must be developed and refined. Without structured training, new coaches often:

  • Struggle to create consistent session outcomes
  • Fail to maintain ethical coaching boundaries
  • Default to advice-giving rather than empowerment
  • Lack the tools to track progress or establish accountability


Coaching training based on ICF principles equips you to:

  • Ask powerful, open-ended questions
  • Build trust and rapport with clients
  • Set and track measurable goals
  • Maintain ethical practices and clear contracts
  • Stay curious, neutral, and client-focused


Love2Coach offers a structured way to learn these foundational coaching skills — all within an easy-to-access online platform built for new and aspiring coaches.
