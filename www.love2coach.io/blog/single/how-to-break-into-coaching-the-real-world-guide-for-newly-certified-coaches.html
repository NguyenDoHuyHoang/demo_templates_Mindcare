How to Break into Coaching: The Real-World Guide for Newly Certified Coaches

You’ve done the work. You’ve completed your coaching certification. You’ve practiced the techniques, embraced the mindset, and maybe even coached a few people during training. But now that you’re certified, a much bigger challenge stands in front of you:

How do you break into coaching for real?

Not just dabble in it. Not just hope for referrals. But actually start coaching paying clients, growing your reputation, and building the career you trained so hard for.

If you’re feeling stuck or unsure about your next move, you’re not alone. Most new coaches find themselves in this exact place—full of potential, but unsure where to begin. In this guide, we’ll walk you through a practical, step-by-step roadmap to break into coaching and introduce you to a powerful platform designed specifically to support coaches just like you: Love2Coach.

Why Breaking into Coaching Feels So Difficult (and Why It Doesn’t Have to Be)

Let’s start by addressing the elephant in the room.

Most coaching programs teach you how to coach—but not how to build a business or find clients. So, once the course ends and the certificate is in your hand, the reality hits:

  • “Where do I find my first paying client?”

  • “Do I need to create a website before I can start?”

  • “Should I be posting on social media? If so, what do I even say?”

  • “Do I need to choose a niche right away?”

  • “How do I explain what I do in a way that attracts people?”

These are common, valid questions. But they often lead to inaction—not because you’re unprepared, but because the path forward is unclear.

That’s where this guide—and the Love2Coach platform—comes in.

Step 1: Shift Your Identity from Student to Coach

The first (and often most overlooked) step is to mentally shift from trainee to professional. You’re no longer “learning to coach.” You are now a coach.

This shift changes everything. It gives you permission to:

  • Talk about your coaching confidently

  • Introduce yourself as a coach in conversations

  • Share your services without feeling like an imposter

Breaking into coaching starts with showing up as a coach—online, offline, and in your own self-image.

Step 2: Simplify Your Offers

You don’t need a five-tier pricing model, complex group programs, or a full course catalogue to get started. You just need one or two simple, clear offers:

  • A single-session option to reduce the barrier for first-time clients

  • A monthly coaching package to support longer transformations

This gives your future clients something tangible to say yes to—and removes the friction of vague offers or unclear pricing.

Step 3: Skip the Website (for Now)

Many new coaches spend weeks—or even months—stuck trying to build a website, design a logo, or decide on brand colours. But none of those things help you find clients today.

Instead, use a ready-made platform that gives you an instant professional presence.

That’s exactly what Love2Coach was built for.

It was created by coaches, for coaches, and is designed to be the simplest and fastest way to break into coaching—especially if you’re just starting out.

Here’s what makes Love2Coach stand out:

  • Completely free to join

  • No commissions—you keep 100% of your earnings

  • No credit card required

  • Built to become the world’s largest coaching marketplace

Let’s look at how it works.

Step 4: Build Your Profile on Love2Coach

When you sign up on www.love2coach.io, you’ll create a public coaching profile that acts as your digital storefront.

Your profile includes:

  • A compelling bio and introduction

  • Coaching services and niche focus

  • Pricing for individual sessions and packages

  • A built-in calendar so clients can book you directly

  • Client reviews and testimonials

  • Optional blog posts or updates to boost your visibility

It’s simple to set up and incredibly effective in positioning you as a professional coach ready to serve.

Step 5: Get Found by Clients Looking for Coaches Right Now

Here’s what most new coaches don’t realise: finding clients isn’t about chasing people. It’s about being findable by people already searching for your services.

Love2Coach does this for you by actively marketing the platform to people seeking coaching in areas like:

  • Wellness

  • Mindset

  • Productivity

  • Career

  • Relationships

  • Health

  • Grief

  • Confidence

  • Business

Visitors to the platform can filter by niche, view your profile, and book with you directly—no funnels, ads, or cold DMs required.

Step 6: Set Your Availability and Get Paid

Once your profile is live, clients can see your availability and book sessions that fit their schedule. You decide how they pay—via Stripe, PayPal, bank transfer—whatever works best for you.

Love2Coach does not touch your money.
 You keep 100% of what you earn. There are no commissions, platform fees, or monthly charges.

This transparency and control are key to building a business you actually enjoy running.

Step 7: Deliver Coaching Sessions Your Way

One of the best things about breaking into coaching through Love2Coach is the flexibility. You coach your clients using the method you prefer:

  • Zoom

  • Google Meet

  • Phone

  • In person (if local)

The platform doesn’t interfere with how you deliver your services. You’re free to create the coaching experience that fits your style and your clients’ needs.

Step 8: Build Your Reputation Through Reviews

Once you’ve coached a few clients, ask them to leave a review on your Love2Coach profile. These public testimonials:

  • Build your credibility

  • Help new clients feel confident booking you

  • Boost your ranking on the platform

The more positive feedback you receive, the more visibility and trust you build—which leads to more bookings.

  • Show up consistently
