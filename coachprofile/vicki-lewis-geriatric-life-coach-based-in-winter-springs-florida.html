Embracing Life’s Changes: Compassionate Geriatric Life Coaching for Seniors

Hi, I’m Vicki Lewis, and I’m here to help seniors embrace life’s changes with confidence, creativity, and a sense of purpose. I’m based in Winter Springs, Florida, and with over 35 years in creative arts and psychology, I understand how the later stages of life can be both challenging and full of opportunity. My mission is simple: to support seniors in living fulfilling, joyful lives, no matter where they are in their journey.

Why I Do What I Do

As we age, life brings so many changes—retirement, health challenges, the loss of loved ones, and sometimes even the feeling of being disconnected from those around us. I know that these changes can feel overwhelming, but I also know that with the right support, this chapter can be incredibly rewarding. As your Geriatric Life Coach, I’m here to be a guide, a listener, and a partner in helping you rediscover your strengths, passions, and the joy that life still has to offer.

How I Can Help You Thrive

I take a holistic approach to coaching, blending creative expression with emotional and mental support. Whether through painting, journaling, or even just conversation, I’ve seen firsthand how creativity helps people cope, reduce stress, and reconnect with themselves. Together, we’ll work on ways to improve your emotional and mental well-being so that you can age gracefully and vibrantly.

Here’s how I can help with some common life transitions:

  • Retirement: Moving from a career to retirement can be hard. I’ll help you explore new passions, find excitement in daily routines, and set new goals that bring meaning to your days.

  • Health Changes: Managing physical health issues like chronic illness can take an emotional toll. Together, we’ll create ways to manage the stress and focus on what you can do to feel better and find peace.

  • Grief and Loss: Losing loved ones is never easy. I’ll walk alongside you, providing emotional support as you work through your grief and find healing in your own time.

  • Social Connection: Loneliness is something many seniors face. We’ll explore ways to build meaningful connections and rekindle a sense of community that helps you feel seen, heard, and loved.

Why Choose Me as Your Geriatric Life Coach?

What makes me different is the genuine, heartfelt care I bring to every coaching session. I don’t just offer tools or advice; I offer a partnership in navigating your life’s journey. With a background in both psychology and the arts, I bring a unique perspective that combines professional expertise with deep empathy. My approach is simple: listen, understand, and support.

Whether you choose in-person or virtual sessions, I’m here to meet you exactly where you are. Together, we’ll work on creating a life full of meaning, growth, and joy, no matter where you’re starting from.

Let’s Take the Next Step Together

If you or a loved one is ready to explore how geriatric life coaching can support a more fulfilling, joyful life, I’d love to connect. Reach out today, and let’s have a conversation about how I can help you live your best life, now and in the years to come.


What I can offer you?

As a certified therapeutic creative arts life coach, I specialize in guiding geriatric clients and their families through a unique blend of creative expression and life coaching techniques designed to enhance their overall well-being, foster emotional resilience, and improve quality of life despite age-related challenges.

  • Clarity Session: The focus is on creating a warm and supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and dreams. Through creative activities like painting, drawing, writing, or gentle movement, you'll have the opportunity to express yourself and gain a deeper understanding of your inner world. Guided prompts will help you uncover any hidden patterns or beliefs that may be holding you back. This process will help you identify what truly matters to you and guide you towards making conscious and meaningful choices in your life..
  • Action Plan: We will create a personalized plan to help you use creative activities for self-discovery and emotional healing. This plan will support you in understanding yourself better, building resilience, and achieving your life goals through creative expression.
  • Personal Growth Check-In: Regular check-ins to assess progress and adjust plans as necessary. Remember, we are a team!
  • Outcomes: Through our collaborative sessions, you will gain clarity on your values and goals, enhance self-awareness, build resilience, and achieve personal growth. Creative expression will help you heal emotionally and transform limiting beliefs into positive actions, leading to meaningful and lasting changes in your life.
  • Not interested in creative arts? No problem at all! We can design a plan that suits your unique preferences and needs. Our coaching sessions are flexible and adaptable, ensuring you feel comfortable and supported every step of the way. Let's discuss the best options for you and create a plan that works perfectly for your journey.
