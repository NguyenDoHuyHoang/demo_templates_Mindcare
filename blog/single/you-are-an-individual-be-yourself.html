You are an individual. Be yourself.

 Paula Gerold       February 24, 2025

Our constitution gives us the inalienable right to life, liberty and the pursuit of happiness. That means we can be ourselves, and being yourself is important when you’re trying to make changes in your life.  Those changes have to easily fit into your life, and this ties back to last week’s concept of making small changes over time.

Again, this sounds easy, doesn’t it?  You CAN do this yourself, but having a program designed to help may be the difference maker.  “But,” you ask, “how can a scripted program work for me as an individual?” Well, a good coach works for the individual and tailors the program to fit.  That’s my approach.  Yes, I have a program, but I let my client lead the session.  The program is my guide and provides me with tools, but our conversations determine where things will go.  I’ve used the same tools with different clients at different times in the program.  The Aha Moments Coaching Program is all about you and what you need. I will come with a plan, but our conversation will guide each session.
